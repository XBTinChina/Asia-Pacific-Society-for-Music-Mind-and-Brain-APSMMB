Welcome to APSMMB

Who We Are

APSMMB is a regional scientific society dedicated to the psychological and neuroscientific study of music. We bring together researchers, clinicians, and practitioners across the diverse nations and cultures of the Asia-Pacific, creating a community that celebrates both scientific rigour and cultural richness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a collaborative and culturally diverse research community by organizing academic seminars and conferences, maintaining an active digital presence, and facilitating member networking and professional development.

Our Scope

APSMMB spans disciplines including music psychology, music neuroscience, music therapy, ethnomusicology, and computational musicology. We embrace the region's extraordinary diversity of musical traditions as both a subject of inquiry and an asset for research.
