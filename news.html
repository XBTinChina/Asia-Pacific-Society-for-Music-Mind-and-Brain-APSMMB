Announcement 20 February 2025

APSMMB Founded

We are proud to announce the founding of the Asia-Pacific Society for Music, Mind, and Brain (APSMMB). Established in 2025 by a group of dedicated researchers from across the region, APSMMB is committed to fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and culturally diverse scientific community for the study of music, mind, and brain. Rooted in the rich cultural landscape of the Asia-Pacific, we aspire to inaugurate a new era of music science that is both globally rigorous and regionally distinctive.
