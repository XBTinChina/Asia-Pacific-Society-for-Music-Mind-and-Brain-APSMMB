Membership Categories

APSMMB welcomes members at all career stages and from all professional backgrounds. Choose the category that best fits your situation.

Category Eligibility Annual Dues Voting Rights
Full Member Researchers, clinicians, or practitioners with active professional or academic interest in music, mind, and brain [TBC] USD Yes
Student Member Currently enrolled graduate or undergraduate students at an accredited institution [TBC] USD (reduced) Advisory
Affiliate Member Professionals from adjacent fields (music education, arts administration, clinical practice) with interest in music science [TBC] USD No
Honorary Member Individuals of distinguished achievement elected by the Executive Committee Waived Honorary

* Dues amounts are to be confirmed by the Executive Committee at the inaugural meeting. Fee waivers and reduced rates may be available — contact membership@apsmmb.org.

Member Benefits

Networking & Directory

Access the APSMMB member directory to find and connect with researchers across the Asia-Pacific. Build collaborations and stay informed about who is doing what in the field.

Event Discounts

Receive discounted registration for all APSMMB events, including the Annual Symposium, seminar series, and workshops. Members are also notified first when registration opens.

Resource Access

Access our curated portal of readings, datasets, analysis tools, funding opportunities, and journal recommendations, designed specifically for music scientists in the Asia-Pacific.

Professional Development

Benefit from mentorship programmes, early-career researcher networks, and targeted resources to support career advancement at every stage, from undergraduate through to senior researcher.

Apply for Membership

Complete the form below to apply for APSMMB membership. Applications are reviewed by the Membership Officer within 14 days. You will be contacted by email with next steps including payment information.

Renew Your Membership

Existing APSMMB members can renew their annual membership by emailing the Membership Officer at membership@apsmmb.org with the subject line "Membership Renewal — [Your Name]". Online renewal via this website will be available in a future update.

Memberships that lapse for more than 60 days will require a new application. Please renew promptly to maintain uninterrupted access to member benefits.
