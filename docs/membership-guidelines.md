# APSMMB Membership Guidelines

## Overview

Membership in the Asia-Pacific Society for Music, Mind, and Brain (APSMMB) is open to individuals with a genuine interest in the psychological and neuroscientific study of music. We welcome researchers, clinicians, educators, practitioners, and students from across the Asia-Pacific region and beyond.

---

## Membership Categories

### Full Member
- **Eligibility**: Researchers, clinicians, or practitioners with an active professional or academic interest in music psychology, music neuroscience, or related fields.
- **Annual Dues**: [TBC] USD
- **Benefits**: Full voting rights, access to all society resources, eligibility to stand for election, discounted event registration, member directory listing.

### Student Member
- **Eligibility**: Currently enrolled graduate or undergraduate students at an accredited institution.
- **Annual Dues**: [TBC] USD (reduced rate)
- **Benefits**: Advisory voting rights, access to society resources, discounted event registration, mentorship programme eligibility, member directory listing.
- **Verification**: Proof of enrolment (e.g., institutional email or student ID) required.

### Affiliate Member
- **Eligibility**: Professionals from adjacent disciplines (e.g., music education, clinical practice, arts administration) with an interest in music science.
- **Annual Dues**: [TBC] USD
- **Benefits**: Access to society resources and events; no voting rights.

### Honorary Member
- **Eligibility**: Individuals of distinguished achievement in music, mind, and brain research, elected by the Executive Committee.
- **Annual Dues**: Waived
- **Benefits**: All Full Member benefits; honorary status does not carry voting rights in standard elections.

## Application Process

1. Visit the [Membership page](../membership.html) on the APSMMB website.
2. Complete the online application form, including:
   - Contact and institutional details
   - Membership category selection
   - Brief statement of research interests (max. 150 words)
   - Agreement to the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
3. Submit the form. The Membership Officer will review your application within 14 days.
4. Upon approval, you will receive a confirmation email with payment instructions for annual dues.
5. Membership is active from the date of payment.

---

## Renewal

Annual membership renews on the anniversary of your joining date. Members will receive a renewal reminder 30 days in advance. Renew by emailing [membership@apsmmb.org](mailto:membership@apsmmb.org) or via the website renewal form when available.

Memberships not renewed within 60 days of the due date will lapse. Former members may re-apply through the standard process.
