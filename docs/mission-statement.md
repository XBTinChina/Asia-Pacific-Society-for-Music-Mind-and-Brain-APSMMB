# APSMMB Mission Statement

The Asia-Pacific Society for Music, Mind, and Brain (APSMMB) exists to foster a collaborative and culturally diverse research community by organizing academic seminars and conferences, maintaining an active digital presence, and facilitating member networking and professional development. Rooted in the rich cultural landscape of the Asia-Pacific, APSMMB aspires to inaugurate a new era of music science that is both globally rigorous and regionally distinctive.

## Core Values

- **Inclusion**: Welcoming researchers at all career stages, from diverse institutional and national backgrounds.
- **Rigour**: Upholding the highest standards of scientific integrity and methodological excellence.
- **Cultural Diversity**: Embracing the unique perspectives that Asia-Pacific cultures bring to the study of music, mind, and brain.
- **Collaboration**: Promoting interdisciplinary and cross-national research partnerships.
- **Openness**: Advocating for open science practices and accessible knowledge dissemination.

## Vision

To be the premier scientific society for music psychology and neuroscience in the Asia-Pacific region, recognized internationally for advancing knowledge that reflects the diversity and richness of regional musical traditions and research perspectives.
