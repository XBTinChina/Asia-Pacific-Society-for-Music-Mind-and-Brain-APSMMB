# Code of Conduct

APSMMB is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all members regardless of nationality, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, or career stage.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate respectfully and professionally in all society interactions
- Acknowledge and cite the contributions of others appropriately
- Support an inclusive research culture that values diverse methodological and cultural perspectives

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation in any form
- Misrepresentation of others' work or credentials
- Disruptive behavior at events or in online forums

## Reporting

Report violations confidentially to: ethics@apsmmb.org

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or removal from the society at the discretion of the Executive Committee.
