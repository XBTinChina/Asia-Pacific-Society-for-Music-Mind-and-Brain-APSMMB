# Asia-Pacific Society for Music, Mind, and Brain (APSMMB)

Official website and repository for the Asia-Pacific Society for Music, Mind, and Brain.

## About

APSMMB is a regional scientific society uniting researchers, clinicians, and practitioners across the Asia-Pacific who share an interest in the psychological and neuroscientific study of music. Our mission is to foster a collaborative and culturally diverse research community by organizing academic seminars and conferences, maintaining an active digital presence, and facilitating member networking and professional development.
